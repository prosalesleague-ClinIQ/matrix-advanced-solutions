Scene 01 — Metabolism

Human Metabolism Landscape

The human body stores energy in adipose tissue. During weight loss, the body breaks down these fat reserves.

mTOR Complex

The central regulator of muscle protein synthesis. Leucine binding triggers a cascade that switches on muscle building.

Ribosome

Molecular machines that assemble amino acids into structural muscle proteins — building blocks of muscle fibers.

Mitochondria

The cell's power generators. Convert nutrients into ATP, fueling metabolism, endurance, and muscle function.

ESD-X3 Dispersion

Electrostatic forces separate nutrient molecules, dramatically increasing surface area and cellular uptake efficiency.
