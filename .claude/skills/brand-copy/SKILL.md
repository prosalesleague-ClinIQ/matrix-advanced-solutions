# Brand Copy Skill

## Voice
Premium, direct, credible, clinic-facing, strategically confident, medically literate, enterprise.

## Sentence style
- Short to medium sentences, clear statements, strong verbs
- High information density, clean structure
- No filler, fluff, or repeated abstractions

## Vocabulary to favor
infrastructure, support, enablement, provider, clinic, category expansion, operational systems, strategic partnership, qualified, premium, enterprise, onboarding, growth

## Vocabulary to avoid
miracle, cure, revolutionary, life-changing, hack, secret, game-changing, guaranteed, explosive growth, insane results, overnight success, no-brainer

## Compliance-safe phrasing
- "supports clinics", "designed for qualified providers"
- "intended for professional audiences"
- "subject to credentialing and review"
- "category access and support"
- Avoid direct medical outcome claims

## CTA patterns
- Request Catalog
- Book Strategy Call
- Start Clinic Onboarding
- Explore Solutions
- Talk to Matrix

## Headline patterns
Declarative, strategic, confident:
- "The Infrastructure Behind Modern Clinic Growth"
- "More Than Supply. Matrix Builds the Infrastructure Around Clinic Growth."

## Required compliance notes (on conversion surfaces)
- "Professional use only"
- "For qualified clinics and providers"
- "Subject to credentialing and approval"
- "Information intended for professional audiences"

## Tone check
Sound expensive and sharp. Never sound loud. Confident, measured, clear, intelligent.
