# Particle Logo Formation Skill

## Formation Strategy
Hybrid system with:
- Far background stars for atmosphere (static, low opacity)
- Guided particles that form the logo shape
- Per-particle swirl parameters for organic motion

## Target Zones
- Lock body (rounded rectangle outline)
- Shackle (top arc)
- Keyhole (center circle + slit)
- Upper helix sweep (DNA strand wrapping lock)
- Lower helix sweep (second DNA strand)
- Orbital rings (atom-like orbits)
- Halo (ambient glow particles)

## Motion Rules
- Start: random scattered positions across viewport
- Phase 1 (0–15%): subtle drift
- Phase 2 (15–60%): swirl acceleration with spiral paths
- Phase 3 (60–95%): convergence toward target positions
- Phase 4 (95–100%): settle with slight overshoot damping
- Swirl intensity peaks in mid-phase, fades during convergence

## Color Transition
- Start: white/blue-white star colors
- End: branded zone colors (indigo lock, purple helix, cyan helix, amber keyhole)

## Particle Count
- Desktop: 1400
- Mobile: 600
- Background stars: 400 desktop, 150 mobile

## Fallback
On weak devices: fewer particles, shorter swirl, clearer silhouette.
